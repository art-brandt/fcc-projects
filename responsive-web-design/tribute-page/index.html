Art group "Kolektivniye Deystviya" (Collectiv Actions)

"Let's go to nature!"

It's time line of Collectiv Actions:

  • 1976 - The group was created by Andrei Monastyrsky. Participants: Nikolai Panitkov, Andrey Abramov, Nikita Alexeev, Igor Makarevich, Elena Elagina, Georgiy Kizevalter, Sergey Romashko,Sabina Hansgen.
  • 1976, on March 13 - The first action "Appearance". These actions became classics of Moscow conceptualism.
  • 1976, on October 2 - action "Tent" in the Moscow region in the forest near the station "Depot" of the Savyolovskaya railway.
  • 1977 - action "The slogan-1977". On the hill between the trees a red cloth (10 mx 1 m) was hung with the inscription in white letters: "I do not complain about anything and I like everything, despite the fact that I have never been here and do not know anything about these places ", which is a quote from the book A. Monastyrsky" Nothing happens. "
  • 1977 - action "Ball" in the Moscow region, on the bank of the Klyazma River near the station "Nazarievo" of the Gorky Railway.
  • 1978 - action "Slogan-1978". On the bank of the river was hung a dark blue cloth (12m x 1m) with the inscription in white letters: "Strangely, why did I lie to myself that I was never there and do not know anything about these places - to do the same thing as I did? As Anywhere, only more acute it's feeling and don't understand. "
  • 1979 - A.Monastyrsky together with I. Kabakov introduced the term "trips from the city", the genre of performance and the books "Collective Actions".
  • 1989 - personal exhibition "Study of documentation" in the exhibition hall "Na Kashirke"
  • 1992 - ersonal exhibition "NOMA or the Moscow Conceptual Circle" in the exhibition center Kunsthalle Hamburg"
  • 2011 - at the 54th Venice Biennale of Contemporary Art, Russia presented the project of Andrei Monastyrsky and the group "Collective Actions" "Empty Zones".
  • By July 2011 - the group held 126 art performances.
  • on 2014 Documentation books of the actions of the group "Travel from the city" includes 12 volumes.
